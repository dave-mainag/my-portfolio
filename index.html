Cancer Screening

This capstone project applies machine learning to predict cancer risk using behavioral health data from the CDC’s BRFSS dataset. After testing several models, XGBoost delivered the best performance and was integrated into a Flask web app on Render. Users input their health data to receive real-time cancer risk predictions, supporting data-driven screening efforts aimed at earlier detection and improved outcomes in low-resource settings.

Polygenic Scoring

This project uses Bash scripting to automate SNP-based risk analysis and Polygenic Risk Score (PRS) computation. It identifies risk alleles, applies GWAS effect sizes to calculate odds ratios, and classifies individual risk levels. For PRS, it aggregates weighted SNPs, normalizes scores, and interprets percentile-based risk. Outputs include personalized genetic risk profiles and health recommendations, ensuring reproducible and efficient genomic risk assessment.
